With 84% of recruitment bosses’ reporting an increase in demand for high profile and temporary/contract roles, recruiters face an uphill battle in convincing clients to pay a little more for contract professionals. Whilst some may see it as a positive – allowing them to bring on top talent with less of a financial commitment – others may well resent the cost.
